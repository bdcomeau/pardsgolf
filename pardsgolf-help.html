Privacy & Data Security

Everything stays on your device

All round data, player names, handicaps, betting results, and Golf Canada member IDs are stored exclusively in your iPhone's browser storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Golf Canada Member ID & Password

Your Golf Canada credentials are used only to fetch your current handicap index. They are stored only on your device, never transmitted to anyone other than Golf Canada's own secure API, and never included in exports or backups.

Pards Golf does not collect analytics, does not serve ads, has no user accounts, and has no server. Your data is yours alone.

1. Installing Pards Golf on Your iPhone

Pards Golf is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Add it to your Home Screen directly from Safari — not from the App Store.

You MUST install to your Home Screen before your data will be reliably saved. Opening in a browser tab means your data can be cleared at any time.
Do NOT use "Remove App" on the Home Screen icon — this permanently wipes all local data.

Step-by-Step Installation

1
Open Safari on your iPhone.
2
Go to: https://bdcomeau.github.io/pardsgolf/pards-golf.html
3
Tap the Share button (square with arrow pointing up, bottom toolbar).
4
Tap "Add to Home Screen."
5
Tap "Add" in the top-right corner.
6
Always launch from the Home Screen icon — not from a Safari bookmark.

Keeping the App Current

Tap Update App in the bottom action bar to check for updates. If still outdated, force-close and reopen, or clear Safari site data for bdcomeau.github.io (Settings → Safari → Advanced → Website Data).

Do NOT delete the Home Screen icon to update — this permanently erases all local data.

2. Setting Up a Round

Step 1 — Choose Your Course

Tap the course picker at the top. Courses are grouped by facility — Derrick is always first. Tap 📋 Info on any course row to see live weather, your history there, and the full scorecard.

Step 2 — Set Number of Players

Use + and − to set 1 to 5 players.

Step 3 — Load Your Favourites

Tap ⭐ Load My Favourites to fill in your saved regular group automatically.

Step 4 — Adding a New Player

  • Type their name, enter their handicap index, select their tee.
  • Optionally enter their 10-digit Golf Canada Member ID.
  • Tap 💾 Save to save as a contact for future rounds.

Step 5 — Fetch Handicaps from Golf Canada

If a Golf Canada ID is entered, tap the Fetch button on their row. First fetch asks for their password once — remembered for all future fetches.

Step 6 — Starting Hole

Defaults to Hole 1. Change for shotgun starts.

Step 7 — Scores Only or With Games?

  • With Games (default) — all betting games available.
  • Scores Only — no betting games.

Step 8 — Birdie Juice Tracking

Toggle 🍺 Birdie Juice Tracking on or off — turn off for rounds with non-drinkers.

Step 9 — Configure Betting Games

Scroll to Betting Games. Tap any accordion header to expand. A green ✓ Active label appears on headers where a game is configured. Pairing pills show full game detail (e.g. "Nassau 2v2 · $2/$2/$2 + $4 Auto Press").

Step 10 — Start the Round

Tap the gold Start Round → button pinned to the bottom of the screen.

Before any score is entered, the scorecard button says "✏️ Edit Round Settings" — tap it to go back to Setup.

3. Scoring — The Scorecard Screen

Entering Scores

Scroll each player's wheel to their score, then tap the centre to confirm. A birdie or better triggers a Birdie Juice popup (if enabled). Correct any score by scrolling again — everything recalculates instantly.

The Settings Button

  • ✏️ Edit Round Settings — before any score is entered. Returns to Setup.
  • ⚙️ Mid-Round Match & Player Settings — once any score is entered. Opens mid-round overlay.

Mid-Round Settings

Change handicaps, switch tee boxes (tap their coloured tee pill), add/remove players, add/adjust any betting game, change starting hole, or toggle Auto-Advance.

Any game can be added mid-round and will back-calculate results for holes already played.

4. Nassau Games

A three-part bet — Front 9, Back 9, Total 18 — each worth one bet unit. Auto-presses tracked automatically.

2v2 Nassau (High-Low)

Two teams of two. Each team contributes their Low ball and High ball net score. Up to three simultaneous 2v2 pairings with 4 players.

1v1 Nassau

One player vs another. Multiple matchups can run simultaneously.

Auto-Press

When a game reaches −2 margin, a press starts from the next hole. Presses can be pressed. All press history shown in the Games tab.

The International (The Big I)

Available after Hole 17 to the losing team. A single Hole 18 can cancel, double, or leave unchanged the entire pot.

  • Losing team wins Hole 18 → pot voided.
  • Leading team wins → pot doubles.
  • Halved → pot stays.

5. The 6-Point Game

Three-player game. Each hole: low net wins 4 pts, second wins 2, third wins 0. Points convert to dollars at round end. Ties for low give 3 pts each.

7. Skins

Each hole is its own contest. Win outright → win the skin. Tie → skin carries over. Select players, set dollars per skin, choose Gross or Net. After 18: Void (default) or Last Hole Sweeps.

With five players at $5/skin, three carried skins on a hole is worth $15 to the winner.

8. Vegas

Two-against-two. Each team's scores combine low digit first (4 and 5 = 45). Teams play the difference per point. The Flip: an opponent birdie flips your number so the high digit leads (45 → 54). Requires 4 or 5 players. Multiple pairings can run simultaneously.

9. The Snake 3-Putt Money Game

Settles every 3 holes. Whoever holds the snake at holes 3, 6, 9, 12, 15, 18 pays each other participant the bet amount. Enable in the 🐍 Snake accordion, set bet per player, select participants.

10. Match Cards

Play 1v1 Nassau against golfers in OTHER groups. Your scores tracked live — opponents' scores entered later in the clubhouse. The ⚔️ Match Cards round lands in History with a purple border and 📝 Score Round pill when your round finishes.

12. The Course Picker

📋 Course Info

Tap 📋 Info on any course row: live weather, your history there (best, avg, lifetime money, last 12 scores vs par), and full 18-hole scorecard with all tee yardages, ratings and slopes.

Tee Colours

Tee pills are colour-coded. Combo tees (Black/Gold, Blue/White, etc.) show as a split diagonal gradient.

Round History on Course Rows

Each course row shows rounds played and last 3 gross scores — visible before you even select the course.

13. Round History

Tap 📜 History in the bottom nav. A floating ‹ Return button appears as you scroll.

  • Saving — tap 💾 Save Round, or rounds save automatically on New Round.
  • Locking — tap 🔓 to lock; hold 🔒 for 2 seconds to unlock.
  • Editing — tap ✏️ Edit on any completed round.
  • Pausing — tap ⚙️ End Round Early → 💾 Save & Pause Round. Resume from History.

14. Backup & Restore

Tap 💾 Backup / Data in the bottom action bar.

  • 📤 Export Backup — saves to Files, iCloud, or email. Named "Pards-Golf-Backup-Jun-28-2026.json."
  • 📥 Import Backup — restores from a previously exported file.
Export a backup regularly. iOS can wipe PWA storage when you remove the Home Screen icon, run low on storage, or during some iOS updates.

15. Starting a New Round

Tap 🔄 New Round in the bottom nav. Hold the confirmation button for 2 seconds. Rounds in progress show a red ⚠️ dialog — hold to abandon, release to cancel.

16. Manage My Players

Tap ⚙️ Manage My Players (below Load My Favourites on Setup):

  • Mark yourself as "Me" — auto-fills Player 1 every round.
  • Star players to mark as Favourites.
  • Set typical score (Par, Bogey, Double) to centre the score wheel.
  • Edit names, handicaps, Golf Canada IDs, tee preferences.

18. Troubleshooting

My round disappeared 🏌️

Use 💾 Save & Pause Round before ending early. Without saving, data cannot be recovered.

The app looks outdated 🤔

Tap Update App. If that doesn't work, clear Safari site data for bdcomeau.github.io, force-close, reopen.

Pop-ups aren't appearing 😶

Run from the Home Screen icon, not a Safari tab.

Player data or history disappeared 😱

Likely caused by "Remove App." Restore from a backup file if available. Always export backups regularly.

A player left mid-round 🚑

Tap ⚙️ Mid-Round Match & Player Settings and remove them there.

A score or game looks wrong 🧮

  • Handicaps editable mid-round in ⚙️ Mid-Round settings.
  • Scores correctable at any time — all totals recalculate instantly.
  • Wrong tee: go to Manage My Players → edit contact → set preferred tee.

Tee box button not responding 📱

Tap anywhere on the dark overlay to dismiss, then try again.

Weather not showing 🌦️

You may be offline. The app works fine without weather.

Handicap strokes wrong ✏️

Formula: Index × (Slope ÷ 113) + (Course Rating − Par), rounded to nearest whole number. Check index, tee, and course are all correct.

History lock not working 🔒

Hold for the full 2 seconds without moving your finger — the icon fades as you hold.
